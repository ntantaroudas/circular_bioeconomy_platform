Welcome to the FUTURAL
Circular Bioeconomy Smart Solution

The platform aims to advance the transition to a circular bioeconomy in the Pongau region (Austria) by providing an integrated platform for exploring, illustrating, and assessing circular bioeconomy knowledge, and opportunities for resource valorisation. The goal is to help the community make informed decisions and identify pathways that enhance resource efficiency, reduce waste, and generate social, environmental, and economic value. Moreover, it provides a guide for the repurposing of underutilized spaces, in line with circular economy principles, thus supporting a more holistic sustainable model of regional development.
