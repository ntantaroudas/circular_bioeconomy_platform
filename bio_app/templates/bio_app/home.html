Welcome to
Circular Bioeconomy Smart Solution

The platform aims to advance the transition to a circular bioeconomy in the Pongau region (Austria) by providing an integrated decision-support tool that illustrates the benefits of alternative resource-valorization pathways. The goal is to help the community make informed choices that enhance resource efficiency, reduce waste, and generate economic, environmental, and social value. Moreover, it provides a guide for the repurposing of underutilized spaces, in line with circular economy principles, thus supporting a more holistic sustainable model of regional development.
